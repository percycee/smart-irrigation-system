    Moisture Thresholds (Science Notes)

    Our thresholds are based on calibration of standard Arduino soil moisture sensors. These sensors output raw values from 0-1023, where lower numbers mean wetter soil. Typical real-world readings: wet (200-400), healthy (400-700), dry (700+). We convert these into a 0-100% scale for the UI:

    • Below 40% -> soil too dry -> auto watering ON
    • 40-80% -> healthy range -> watering OFF
    • Above 80% -> oversaturated -> watering blocked
